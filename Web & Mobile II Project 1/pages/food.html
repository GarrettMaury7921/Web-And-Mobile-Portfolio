Old Ellicott City!

Food in Old Ellicott City!

La Palapa!

La Palapa is a Mexican Grill and Cantina that you can find as a restaurant in Old Ellicott City. Many waiters speak spanish so it is also a good place to practice spanish! Some high schools bring their spanish class to have the students sometimes speak to the waiters in spanish!

They have very good food that range from quesadillas to chips and nachos!

They also host private music events and have many other food options! You can eat outside and inside!




Old Mill Cafe!

Old Mill Cafe is more of a coffee shop located in Old Ellicott City. They have good doughnuts, coffee, and other breaded cuisines.

Old Mill Cafe also serves breakfast that includes Eggs with cheese, english muffins, and toasted croissants with melted ham.
